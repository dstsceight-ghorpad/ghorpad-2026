Notes & Assumptions

1. Currency Conversion: All USD to INR conversions use an approximate rate of ₹84 = $1 USD. Actual costs may vary based on prevailing exchange rates at the time of payment.

2. Vercel Pro Requirement: Vercel's Terms of Service require a Pro plan ($20/month) for any commercial, organizational, or institutional website. The Hobby (free) plan is restricted to personal, non-commercial use only. This is a non-negotiable cost for institutional deployment.

3. Supabase Free Tier Limitations: The free tier is viable for initial launch. However, Supabase may pause inactive projects on the free plan after 7 days of inactivity. The Pro plan ($25/month) eliminates this risk and provides daily backups, which is strongly recommended for any production institutional website.

4. Development Cost: The website has been designed and developed in-house by the editorial team at zero external development cost. Equivalent market cost for this scope of development would be approximately ₹2,00,000 – ₹5,00,000 if outsourced to a professional web development agency.

5. Scalability: The chosen technology stack (Next.js + Vercel + Supabase) is enterprise-grade and used by major organizations worldwide. It can handle growth from hundreds to tens of thousands of monthly visitors without architecture changes. Cost increases would only come from storage and bandwidth overages, which are billed incrementally.

6. Payment Methods: Vercel and Supabase accept international credit/debit cards (Visa/Mastercard). Domain registrars (BigRock, GoDaddy India) accept Indian payment methods including UPI, net banking, and domestic debit cards.

7. Annual Review: Service pricing is subject to change. It is recommended to review costs annually and compare with alternative providers. All services mentioned offer month-to-month billing with no long-term contracts.

8. No Hidden Costs: There are no additional licensing fees for the software framework (Next.js is open-source under MIT license), UI libraries, or development tools used in building this website.
